How Wordle Bot works

Enter your last guess and match the tile colors to Wordle. Tap Best next guess for recommendations and remaining answer lists.

After each guess you should see something like this:

T is in the correct position, A is in the word but not in the correct position, and R, I, and N are not in the word.

  • 1. BLOKE
    2.188 guesses left
  • 2. YOLKS
    2.250 guesses left
  • 3. KOELS
    2.250 guesses left
  • 4. KYLOE
    2.250 guesses left

This means the best guess from this point would be BLOKE, and that you have an average of 2.188 guesses left. If you see:

  • 1. BLOKE
    96.77% solve rate

That means BLOKE will only solve 96.77% of the remaining possible answers within 6 guesses. Generally speaking, you should only see this if you're playing on hard mode.

Wordle Bot

Wordle Bot is a free Wordle solver and Wordle hint tool. Type your last guess, match the green and yellow tiles to Wordle, then tap Best next guess. You will see the best next words plus probable and unlikely answers still in play.

Settings (top right): pick how many letters per word, how many turns you get, whether to use the standard Wordle answer list or a larger dictionary, and special variants like Dordle or Warmle.

Benchmark (top right): enter a starting word (for example CRANE) and run a full test against random answers to see how strong that opener is, with live stats and a guess-by-guess simulation.
